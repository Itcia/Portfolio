This UX/UI project was developed for a regulated Canadian immigration consultant specializing in assisting workers, students, and visitors with their visa applications. The primary objective was to design a user-friendly and efficient system to streamline the immigration process for clients.

Project Objectives:

  • Optimized Time Tracking: A system that helps track and manage the time spent on each task, ensuring that both the client and consultant are aware of deadlines and important milestones in the immigration process.
  • Efficient Document Management: A feature designed to allow users to upload, organize, and manage required documents efficiently, minimizing errors and ensuring that everything is in place for visa applications.
  • Tracking Reports: Real-time reports that track the progress of each application, providing transparency for both clients and consultants regarding where they stand in the immigration process.
  • Provide Detailed Information To Make Progress Clear: A clear display of all necessary details and updates for users, making the process easy to understand and ensuring that clients are always informed of what’s happening with their application.
  • Indicators To Monitor The Process: Visual progress bars or status indicators, that help users track how far along they are in the application process and what steps remain.
  • Track Financial Data: A system to manage and track payments and financial records, ensuring that clients can keep up with their fees and consultants can monitor their revenue streams.
  • Communication System Between Client And Consultant: A built-in messaging or communication feature that allows seamless interaction between clients and consultants, improving collaboration and reducing misunderstandings.
  • Notification System: A system that sends automatic alerts or reminders to both clients and consultants about important updates, deadlines, or actions required to keep the process moving smoothly.

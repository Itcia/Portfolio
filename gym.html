UX Research

For this project, I undertook the design and development of a comprehensive website for a gym. The project aimed to address specific issues and improve the overall user experience. Key components of the project included:

  • Membership Management: Developed an efficient system for users to sign up, renew, and manage their memberships online, streamlining the process and reducing administrative overhead.
  • Activity Schedules: Created a dynamic section to provide up-to-date information on various activities and classes, along with schedules and instructor details, ensuring members could easily find and participate in their preferred activities.
  • Location Information: Designed detailed pages for the gym’s multiple locations, including addresses, contact details, and facilities available at each site, helping members and potential customers find the most convenient location.
  • Online Store: Integrated an e-commerce platform for users to browse and purchase gym products and merchandise, enhancing the gym’s revenue streams and providing convenience for members.
  • Personalized User Profiles: Implemented a system for users to create and manage personalized profiles, offering tailored workout plans, activity tracking, and personal goals to enhance engagement and user satisfaction.

This project focused on creating a user-friendly, visually appealing website that makes essential information easily accessible and provides a personalized experience for gym members. The design process involved user research, wireframing, prototyping, and usability testing to ensure the final product met the needs and expectations of the users.
